Frequently Asked Questions

1. How does this system work?

Step 1. Upload your current home address and work address along with the home images.
Step 2. We will show you all the new homes that are closer to your working location.
Step 3. Click to like a home If you think you may want to live in that house.
Step 4. If the homeowner of the home you liked also likes your current home, then there is a match!
Step 5. We will notify you the match, and it's your decision whether to move forward, like visiting or even signing the contract


2. How will I be protected through the process

First, our system will check the background of the user to ensure there is no criminal history.
Second, we provide the house switching contract so that you are protected by the law.
Third, the money that you pay for the other side will be storing in our system, and won't be paid to the other side until you confirm you have moved in.


3. How do I pay for the rent after switch

The amount of the rent is entirely up to the home owners. If both sides feel that the switch is a fair trade, then they can just switch for free.
If there is money involved in the switching, then the user can pay by the bank accounts, PayPal, credit cards, and also debit cards.
